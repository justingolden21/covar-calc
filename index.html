Formula:

$$COV(x,y) = \frac{ \sum_{i=1}^n (x_i - \bar x)(y_i - \bar y) }{n-1}$$

COvariance refers to the relationship between two variables. If it's positive, they're directly correlated, if it's negative they're inversly correlated. The larger the absolute value of the covariance, the stronger the relationship between the variables
